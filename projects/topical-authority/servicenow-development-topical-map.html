Executive Summary

▼

ServiceNow Development represents a high-value, growing market with strong career prospects and enterprise demand. More than 80% of Fortune 500 companies use ServiceNow, creating consistent demand for skilled developers. The topic spans scripting (GlideRecord, Business Rules, Client Scripts), low-code development (Flow Designer, App Engine Studio), integrations (REST APIs, IntegrationHub), and emerging AI/GenAI capabilities (Now Assist).
